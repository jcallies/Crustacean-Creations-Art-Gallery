Hi, Everybody!!

One-of-a-kind seashell artworks and paintings are designed by Josh Phresh, artist and owner.

A curious and creative type, Josh lived in Wisconsin most of his life and then gave up on winters and moved to Florida. The Sunshine State has certainly given him a new appreciation for the wonders of nature, particularly our oceans and our sea life.

Josh is inspired by and creates for his beautiful girlfriend Maria and his dopy dog Sun Drop (aka, “Sunny Boy”).
