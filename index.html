Shellebrate Good Finds - Come On!

Welcome to Crustacean Creations, a contemporary art gallery featuring hand-crafted artwork sculpted from beach debris found along the Gulf of Mexico.

Located in St. Petersburg, FL, Crustacean Creations’ artwork includes wall hangings, statuettes, and oversized postcards with a vintage, “Old Florida” feel – a Crustacean Creations specialty.

Each piece of art is custom-designed and one-of-a-kind, with shells and other debris hand-picked directly from the beach by the artist himself.

Why Seashells?

Seashells lie in abundance on Florida beaches, waiting for the watchful beachcomber to come along, discover, and collect them. Seashells have all it takes to trigger a collection compulsion – beauty, variety, mystery, and a pocket-sized form.

Seashells also happen to be scattered at my favorite place, where a barefoot, sandy-seaside stroll can reveal a bounty of collectibles, even during a casual visit. And, for a searcher with eyes filled with visions of future artworks, there is a diverse world spiced with rare and provocative shells that most others pass by.

What does it mean to "Shellebrate Good Finds!"

Strolling along the shore, stepping carefully through the rows upon rows of thousands upon thousands of glistening shells below your toes, when one shell stands out above all the rest. Maybe because it’s bright, vibrant. Maybe it’s worn, sun-bleached. Maybe it’s a cat paw or an olive, a coquina or a conch, a scallop or an auger or a cockle or a turkey wing or a lightning whelk or a spotted slipper or a spiny jewelbox or a “plain” ol’ jingle shell.

Whatever it is that shows its beauty, whatever it is that makes you smile, whatever it is that causes you to bend down, pick it up, give it a rinse and slip it into your pocket, that’s a “good find” to me. So come on, and Shellebrate Good Finds!
